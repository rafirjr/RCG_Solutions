Successes

Throughout this project we were extremely successful with the CAD designs, website development, and Tier 1 of the RC car project. The CAD designs were made almost to perfection and all FEA simulations were done to the necessary part files. The website looks clean, smooth, and details all of our progress through this extensive project. For the RC car project, we were successfully able to wire the bluetooth connection to the breadboard and were able to send commands to the RC car. After some calibrations, the RC car made it through Maze 2 while carrying a ping pong ball and was ultimately able to set it down at the end of the maze.

Challenges

Unfortunately, we were not able to complete the entirety of the project due to the challenges we faced with Tier 2 and Tier 3 of the RC car project. For Tier 2, we were not able to wire the IR receiver correctly and therefore could not send signals from the controller to the car. As for tier 3, we simply did not have enough time to get a solid block of autonomous code for the car to get through the maze even though our wiring with the sensor was working. These challenges helped us realize that we should have planned ahead for unexpected difficulties.
